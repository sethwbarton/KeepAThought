What is Keep A Thought?

Easy, Simple, Social, Pure.

On Keep A Thought, you don't have to deal with ads, you don't have to post what you write, you don't even have to look at what other people are writing - but you can if you want.

Write What You're Thinking.

You can write right away! Just make an account and you're in action. You can keep a thought personal so no one can read it, or make it public so those who follow you can see it.

It's Not a Bug, It's a Feature!

The thoughts you write on our site stay in our servers, so you don't have to worry about them ever getting lost. You can also print them for you're own use, download them, and share them to other social platforms. Make your thoughts your own with a wide variety of fonts and styling tools.
